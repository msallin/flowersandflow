The Art

What is Poi?

An ancient art form rooted in Māori culture, Poi combines rhythm, coordination, and creative expression.

🌀

A Living Tradition

Poi originates from the Māori people of Aotearoa (New Zealand). Traditionally, it was used to develop flexibility and strength in the hands and arms, as well as to tell stories through movement and song.

Today, Poi has evolved into a global art form embraced by flow artists, dancers, and performers around the world. From contact poi to fire poi, the practice continues to grow and inspire.

Why Spin Poi?

Poi offers a unique blend of physical exercise, meditative focus, and creative expression. Whether you're drawn to the cultural roots, the mesmerizing visual patterns, or simply the joy of movement — there's something in Poi for everyone.
